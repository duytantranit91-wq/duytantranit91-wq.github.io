We, Hangzhou AtlasV Technology Co., Ltd. and its affiliates, are committed to protecting your privacy. We limit the collection of your data — we only collect data that is required to maintain Period Tracker. This Privacy Policy does not apply to data you send and/or receive from third-party websites you may process through Period Tracker.

1
Collection of Data
1.1 Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect the following data:

  • Names (including subscriber names, user names, and screen names)
  • Addresses (including mailing, residential, and business addresses)
  • Telephone numbers
  • Payment details — we do not collect or store credit card numbers, bank account numbers, or billing records
1.2 Data We Collect

We only collect data that is required to maintain Period Tracker.

1.2.1 Data That You Give Us

We collect data you provide through your interaction with Period Tracker. If you contact us directly, we may receive data such as your email address and the content of your message.

We use your email to:

  • Communicate with you
  • Send you updates and announcements
  • Send marketing information such as offers, surveys, and invitations

You may withdraw your consent to our use of your email address at any time.

1.2.2 Data Necessary to Maintain Quality
  • App version data — helps us efficiently identify and eliminate technical issues you may face.
  • Health & sensitive data — such as body measurements, dates of past and current periods, and symptoms or events you track (e.g. sex, productivity levels, pain, or cravings). This data is used exclusively to provide our services to you.
  • Social sign-in data — if you sign up via Facebook/Google, we collect your basic profile info (name, email, profile picture) under the terms of their respective privacy policies. You may control this in your Facebook/Google account settings.
  • Anonymous diagnostic data — anonymized crash reports, network failures, and service diagnostics to operate Period Tracker reliably.
2
Use of Data
2.1 Security

We authenticate your use of Period Tracker by assigning a unique identifier to your device — without reference to any other personal data. We do not use other forms of your data to verify your identity.

2.2 System Diagnostics

We use your usage data for troubleshooting, data analysis, and activity trend analysis to maintain Period Tracker's reliability.

2.3 Customer Support

We use your data to resolve customer support issues, improve our responses, answer questions, and communicate updates and promotional information.

2.4 Research & Development

We analyze usage patterns to develop new features, services, and improvements to Period Tracker.

3
Security Measures

We implement robust security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, modification, or similar risks. Our staff's access to your data is restricted to a need-to-know basis. We do not collect or store any traffic data or personal data in our data centers.

4
Data Retention

We retain your data only while we have an ongoing legitimate business need — such as providing a requested service or complying with legal requirements.

When there is no ongoing need, we will delete or anonymize it. If deletion is temporarily not possible (e.g. backup archives), the data will be securely stored and isolated until it can be deleted.

5
Right to Be Forgotten

We will erase your data when:

  • Your data is no longer necessary for the purpose it was collected
  • You withdraw your consent and we are relying on that consent as the legal basis for processing
  • You object to processing and there is no overriding legitimate interest to continue
  • We are processing your data for direct marketing and you object to that processing
6
Orders Compelling Disclosure & GDPR

Our data collection is limited — we do not collect IP addresses, browsing history, traffic data, or DNS queries. We may be compelled to disclose your data only under a valid court order enforceable in Singapore, or where legally required. Recipients must protect your data to a standard at least equivalent to Singapore's PDPA.

6.1 GDPR

For users in the EU, we process data under the following legal bases:

  • Fulfilling contractual obligations: providing services, managing subscriptions, and customer support
  • Legitimate interests: improving service quality and communicating with users

You may withdraw consent at any time, opt out of marketing via the "unsubscribe" link in our emails, and exercise your rights to access, transfer, correct, or delete your data by contacting us at [email protected].

7
Children's Privacy

We do not offer Period Tracker to children. If you are under 13 years old, you may only use Period Tracker with the consent, involvement, and supervision of a parent or guardian.

8

9
